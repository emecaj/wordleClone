 How to Play

Can YOU guess the five-letter mystery word?

Press the keys on your screen or use your keyboard to type in a word that you think is the mystery word. When you are ready to submit your guess, press the check mark on the screen or hit 'ENTER' on your keyboard. Only valid words can be submitted!

Once your word is submitted, the color of the tiles will change to show you how close you were to guessing the mystery word!
